About us

As a nonprofit organization, you likely rely heavily on donations to fund your programs and support your mission. 
    
    
In today’s digital age, donation pages have become an essential component of nonprofit fundraising efforts,
 allowing organizations to reach a wider audience and accept online donations. However, not all nonprofit donation pages are created equal, and some may be more effective than others in generating donations and engaging supporters. 
In this blog, we’ll explore the importance of donation pages for nonprofits and provide insights into how your organization can optimize your donation pages to achieve fundraising success.
 Plus, we have a few stellar nonprofit donation page examples to inspire you as you create new pages or build on your existing ones!!

  
This donation page includes a plugin from Double the Donation that allows users to search their company name. Once the donation is submitted, an automated email will go out to the company requesting the matching gift.
